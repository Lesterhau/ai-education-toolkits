## What's in them

Of the 34 state AI education documents currently in 
circulation, zero include a named implementation role with 
a job description, a student data privacy vendor framework, 
or a succession protocol. These do.

Each toolkit includes: a named leadership role with 30-day 
action plan, three-zone AI use policy, student data privacy 
requirements, AI-Free Declaration Track for students with 
principled ethical objections, succession planning, and 
Texas-specific political risk analysis.
